Inverted Index
It is an data structure.Most operational Information retrieval system are based on Information Retrieval.As the word says, an index maps back the term to the document where they occur .We keep a dictionary of terms ,it is the basic idea of the inverted index .Using the document Id,Posting list was  sorted and In alphabetical order the dictionary was sorted.
A posting list or inverted list is a list that consists of records of the term occurrence in the documents.Each item in list is posting.Inverted Index becomes standard term in information retrieval .In simple words, it is a hashmap like data structure that directs you from a word to a document or a web page.
