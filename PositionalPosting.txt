Positional Postings and Non Positional Postings
In the posting list ,the positions at which the term appears in a particular document is also stored along with the docID.It helps to make the retrieval process faster,but occupy more space .Non positional postings are normal postings which have term , ID and frequency of term.It is typically sorted by the document ID and stored in the form of a linked list.In Python data format containing a dictionary and linked lists to store the postings list.It occupies bit lesser space than the positional postings but makes the retrieval process a bit slower.
